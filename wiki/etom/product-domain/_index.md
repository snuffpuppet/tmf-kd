# eTOM — Product Domain (Operational L2s)

L2 process groups in the Product Domain × OFAB verticals. Source: GB921 v25.5 Excel
master.

> **Commercial-side relevance.** Product Domain operational processes are the
> commercial-layer operations: customer-visible product order handling, billing,
> usage, balance, and product catalog operations. These processes manipulate Product
> ABE entities ([[wiki/sid/product-abe]]) and pair with CustomerFacingService work
> in the Service Domain — completing the production/commercial separation pattern.
