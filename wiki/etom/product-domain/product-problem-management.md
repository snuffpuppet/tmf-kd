# Product Problem Management

> **Vertical context:** Assurance ([[wiki/foundations/lifecycle]]).
> **Domain:** Product Domain ([[wiki/foundations/domains#Product Domain]]).

## Overview

Product Problem Management process is in charge of directing and controlling activities that identify, triage, discover causes, report and close product problems (cases, issues, challenges and faults etc.). 

Product Problem Management ensures that products that are commercially offered by the organization can be assured to meet the goals and objectives of their design. The objective of this processes is to respond to product challenges/concerns/issues/cases and offer answers/resolution to them. 

The goal of this process is mediate the impact, or resolve the concerns that impact Marketing and Sales objectives.

— GB921 v25.5 Excel master, process ID `1.2.26`, sheet `eTOM25,5`.

## L3 Process Details

Per-L3 Extended Descriptions and L4 sub-process listings, verbatim from GB921 v25.5 Excel master. The L2 Overview above gives the L2-level brief; this section provides the L3 narrative depth and the L4 hierarchy.

### 1.2.26.1 Identify Product Problem

**Extended Description.**

Identify Product Problem business activity is in charge of establishing a product case/concern/issues or challenge.

Identify Product Problem includes activities that clearly define product problem and collect information that relevantly described the existence of the problem.

This business activity may include tasks that measure or assess problem to determine whether a problem (i.e., discrepancy) exists.

**L4 sub-processes:**

- **1.2.26.1.1** Define Product Problem — Define Product Problem business activity is in charge of describing a product problem.
- **1.2.26.1.2** Collect Product Problem Information — Collect Product Problem Information business activity is in charge of gathering all data/information that enable establish a product problem.

### 1.2.26.2 Triage Product Problem

**Extended Description.**

Triage Product Problem business activity is in charge of organizing product problems based on business defined conditions to support analyzing and managing problem closure.

Triage Product Problem includes tasks that categorize product problems in order to effectively prioritize handling according to their impact on business.

**L4 sub-processes:**

- **1.2.26.2.1** Categorize Product Problem — Categorize Product Problem business activity is in charge of organizing and classifying product problems based on business conditions.
- **1.2.26.2.2** Prioritize Product Problem Management — Prioritize Product Problem business activity is in charge of ordering product problems by level of urgency and impact based to the business.

### 1.2.26.3 Analyze Product Problem

**Extended Description.**

Analyze Product Problem business activity is in charge of investigating product problems, discovering product problem cause and providing relevant reports to outline problem product cause.

This business activity is key to identify appropriate solutions to product problems, and inform the right plan of action to resolve product problems.

**L4 sub-processes:**

- **1.2.26.3.1** Investigate Product Problem — Investigate Product Problem business activity is in charge of observing and identifying the cause of a product problem.
- **1.2.26.3.2** Report Product Problem Cause — Report Product Problem Cause business activity in charge of providing documented (written or verbal) evidence of the cause of a product problem.

### 1.2.26.4 Manage Product Problem Solution

**Extended Description.**

Manage Product Problem Solution business activity is in charge of directing and controlling activities that establish solutions to resolve product problems.

Manage Product Problem Solution includes activities that identify and define all product problem solutions, establish course of action for resolving product problems according to solutions, reviewing product problem solutions to be effective, implement established plans for resolving or correcting product problems and reporting on implementation of product problem solutions.

**L4 sub-processes:**

- **1.2.26.4.1** Define Product Problem Solution — Define Product Problem Solution business activity is in charge of finding and describing solutions to the product problem.
- **1.2.26.4.2** Plan Product Problem Solution — Plan Product Problem Solution business activity is in charge of identifying tasks and actions that enable implement answers or correction to a product problem.
- **1.2.26.4.3** Implement Product Problem Solution — Implement Product Problem Solution business activity is in charge of executing the set of tasks identified to address or correct product problem.
- **1.2.26.4.4** Review Product Problem Solution — Review Product Problem Solution business activity is in charge of inspecting and authorizing a product problem solution.
- **1.2.26.4.5** Report Product Problem Solution — Report Product Problem Solution business activity is in charge of documenting (written or verbal) the answer or corrective actions applied to resolve or mediate a product problem.

### 1.2.26.5 Close Product Problem

**Extended Description.**

Close Product Problem business activity is in charge of finalizing all activities that resolve or correct a product problem and updating product problem knowledge base to formally end a product problems lifecycle.

**L4 sub-processes:**

- **1.2.26.5.1** Update Product Problem Knowledge Management — Update Product Problem Knowledge Management business activity is in charge of adding or enhancing the product problem knowledge-based with all new activities and related information about the instance of product problem management.
