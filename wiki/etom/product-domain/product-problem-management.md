# Product Problem Management

> **Vertical context:** Assurance ([[wiki/foundations/lifecycle]]).
> **Domain:** Product Domain ([[wiki/foundations/domains#Product Domain]]).

## Overview

Product Problem Management process is in charge of directing and controlling activities that identify, triage, discover causes, report and close product problems (cases, issues, challenges and faults etc.). 

Product Problem Management ensures that products that are commercially offered by the organization can be assured to meet the goals and objectives of their design. The objective of this processes is to respond to product challenges/concerns/issues/cases and offer answers/resolution to them. 

The goal of this process is mediate the impact, or resolve the concerns that impact Marketing and Sales objectives.

— GB921 v25.5 Excel master, process ID `1.2.26`, sheet `eTOM25,5`.

## L3 Processes

The following L3 processes decompose this L2. Verbatim from GB921 v25.5:

- **1.2.26.1** Identify Product Problem — Identify Product Problem business activity is in charge of establishing a product case/concern/issues or challenge.
- **1.2.26.2** Triage Product Problem — Triage Product Problem business activity is in charge of organizing product problems based on business defined conditions to support analyzing and managing problem closure.
- **1.2.26.3** Analyze Product Problem — Analyze Product Problem business activity is in charge of investigating product problems, discovering product problem cause and providing relevant reports to outline problem product cause.
- **1.2.26.4** Manage Product Problem Solution — Manage Product Problem Solution business activity is in charge of directing and controlling activities that establish solutions to resolve product problems.
- **1.2.26.5** Close Product Problem — Close Product Problem business activity is in charge of finalizing all activities that resolve or correct a product problem and updating product problem knowledge base to formally end a product problems lifecycle.
