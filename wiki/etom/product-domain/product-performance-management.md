# Product Performance Management

> **Vertical context:** Assurance ([[wiki/foundations/lifecycle]]).
> **Domain:** Product Domain ([[wiki/foundations/domains#Product Domain]]).

## Overview

Product Performance Management business process manage activities that define and manage product success based on user expectations, user reception, and user satisfaction in line with product design goals (feature set, product reliability, product conformance, product durability, product serviceability, aesthetics, and perceived quality). Product Performance Objectives (e.g. Profitability, Speediness, Time to market, Product Adoption Rate, Product Revenue, Units Sold,  Product Quality, Average Purchase Value, etc.) establish performance goals & targets, track product performance trends, monitor performance, analyze performance, control performance (optimize, troubleshoot), report performance, and manage consequences of performance. Product Performance Management helps product innovation and interworks with Enterprise Performance Management to address competitiveness and Enterprise performance needs. Through Product Performance Management business process, Products are offered and managed to meet the Enterprise Performance Management goals. Product Performance Management address quality, efficiency, productivity, and profitability mandates by identifying and establishing the applicable methodologies that define and manage performance criteria to meet Market & Sales Performance objectives.

## L3 Processes

The following L3 processes decompose this L2. Verbatim from GB921 v25.5:

- **1.2.6.10** Establish Product Performance Goals — Undertaking proactive monitoring regimes of groups of similar purchased product offerings as required to ensure ongoing performance within agreed parameters over time.
- **1.2.6.11** Monitor Product Performance — Monitor Product Performance business activity checks and logs product performance.
- **1.2.6.12** Analyze Product Performance — Analyze Product Performance business activity aggregates, provides research findings and product performance reports.
- **1.2.6.13** Control Product Performance — Control Product Performance business activity determine and administer changes and directives the manage product performance accomplishment.
- **1.2.6.14** Report Product Performance — Report Product Performance Analysis business activity provides detailed account of research findings and outcome of evaluation of product performance.
