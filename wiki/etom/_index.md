# eTOM

In-scope L2 process groups from GB921 v25.5. Strategy, Infrastructure, and Product
Lifecycle Management verticals are out of scope per CLAUDE.md §3 (the SIP/S2R
verticals).

The corpus filters L2s by:
- **Domain** ∈ {Product, Service, Resource}
- **Vertical** ∈ {Operations Readiness & Support, Fulfillment, Assurance, Billing} (OFAB)

Source: GB921 v25.5 Excel master (`raw/tmf/etom/GB921_Business_Process_Framework_Processes_Excel_v25.5.xlsx`).
