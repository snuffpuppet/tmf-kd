# eTOM — Resource Domain L2s

L2 process groups in the Resource Domain across both Lifecycle Areas defined in GB991
§1.1.2: **Operations** (OFAB — Operations Readiness & Support, Fulfillment, Assurance,
Billing) and **Strategy-to-Readiness** (Strategy Management, Capability Management,
Business Value Development). Source: GB921 v25.5 Excel master.

> **Production-side relevance.** Resource Domain processes are the production-layer
> operations: how the network/infrastructure is provisioned, monitored, repaired,
> and accounted. These processes manipulate Resource entities
> ([[wiki/sid/resource-abe]]) and pair with ResourceFacingService work in the
> Service Domain.

> **Phase 3 expansion note.** The Strategy-to-Readiness verticals were originally
> excluded per CLAUDE.md §3 (SIP vertical out of scope). The Phase 3 expansion
> (decisions locked 2026-05-10) brings them into scope to support the user's
> current → target transformation roadmap framing. CLAUDE.md §3 amendment is
> pending. S2R L2s are added to the table below as they are ingested.
