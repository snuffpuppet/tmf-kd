# Product ABE

Category landing page. Lists Product ABE entities ingested from GB922 Product
v25.5. This page is not normative for individual ABEs — entity-level normative
content lives in the per-ABE pages linked below.

## ABEs in this category

GB922 Product v25.5 organises the Product Domain into 12 top-level ABEs
(§4.1–§4.12 of the source):

- [[wiki/sid/product/product-party-roles-abe]] — PartyRoles in the Product Domain
  (ProductUser, ProductManager, TaxJurisdiction, LoyaltyProgramMember)
- [[wiki/sid/product/product-specification-abe]] — ProductSpecification and its
  atomic/composite/sub-spec entities (Goods & Shipping, Network, Usage Volume)
- [[wiki/sid/product/product-offering-specification-abe]] — ProductOfferingSpecification,
  Pricing Logic Algorithm, Product Catalog, Product Offering Price (and Rule, Tax)
- [[wiki/sid/product/product-and-offering-instance-abe]] — Product and ProductOfferingInstance
  (the inventory layer)
- [[wiki/sid/product/product-order-abe]] — ProductOrder (Customer, BusinessPartner) and
  order item entities
- [[wiki/sid/product/product-usage-abe]] — Usage abstract base; ProductUsage,
  ServiceUsage, ResourceUsage; UsageSpecification
- [[wiki/sid/product/loyalty-abe]] — LoyaltyProgramProdSpec, LoyaltyAccount,
  LoyaltyProgramProduct, LoyaltyRule, LoyaltyBurn
- [[wiki/sid/product/product-configuration-abe]] — ProductConfiguration applying the
  generic Configuration pattern from Common Domain
- [[wiki/sid/product/product-test-abe]] — Test, ProductTest, TestSpecification,
  TestMeasure
- [[wiki/sid/product/product-capacity-abe]] — Capacity, CapacityAmount,
  CapacityDemand, ApplicableTimePeriod
- [[wiki/sid/product/product-performance-abe]] — *«notFullyDeveloped»* per source
- [[wiki/sid/product/strategic-product-portfolio-plan-abe]] — *«notFullyDeveloped»* per source

For full structural overview see [[wiki/sid/product/_index]] and the source PDF.
