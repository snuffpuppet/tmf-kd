# Service ABE

Category landing page. Lists Service ABE entities ingested from GB922 Service v25.0.
This page is not normative for individual ABEs — entity-level content lives in the
per-ABE pages linked below.

> **Production/commercial relevance.** The Service Domain houses the **CFS/RFS
> distinction** — Customer Facing Service (commercial layer, bound to Products) vs
> Resource Facing Service (production/technical layer, bound to Resources). This is
> directly the separation the corpus's PSR mapping goal targets.

## ABEs in this category

GB922 Service v25.0 organises the Service Domain into 12 top-level ABEs (§4.2–§4.13
of the source):

- [[wiki/sid/service/service-party-roles-abe]] — ServiceDesigner, TechnicalSolutionDesigner
- [[wiki/sid/service/service-specification-abe]] — **CFSSpec/RFSSpec**, ServiceCatalog, plus 8 nested sub-ABEs
- [[wiki/sid/service/service-abe]] — **CFS/RFS instances**, ServiceRole, plus nested sub-ABEs
- [[wiki/sid/service/service-configuration-abe]] — Service-side specialisation of canonical Configuration
- [[wiki/sid/service/service-order-abe]] — ServiceOrder, decomposes ProductOrder into Services
- [[wiki/sid/service/service-usage-abe]] — ServiceUsage, ServiceUsageSpec
- [[wiki/sid/service/service-test-abe]] — Service-side specialisation of canonical Test
- [[wiki/sid/service/service-performance-abe]] — SLS, SLA, KPI/KQI, ServiceLevelSpec entities
- [[wiki/sid/service/service-capacity-abe]] — Service-side specialisation of canonical Capacity
- [[wiki/sid/service/service-problem-abe]] — *«NotFullyDeveloped»* per source
- [[wiki/sid/service/service-strategy-and-plan-abe]] — *«notFullyDeveloped»* per source
- [[wiki/sid/service/tip-service-management-abe]] — *«likelyToBeDepreciated»* per source (flagged for removal in Release 25.5)
